Implementing a Bit of Scrabble with Drag-and-Drop

Scrabble

Drag a tile from the rack onto the board.

If there are no gaps between tiles on the board, you word will be accepted.

To submit a word, click the Submit Word button if your word is longer than one character.

You can select new tiles at any time by clicking the New Tiles button.

If you want to start a new game, click the Reset Game button.
